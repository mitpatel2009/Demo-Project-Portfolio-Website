Catcher Game

You can move the catcher left and right using arrow keys.
You have total 3 lives. If you catch one ball your score increases by one
and if you catch 20 balls before losing 3 of your life you win.
If you lose the 3 life before scoring 20 you lose

Ball and Paddle GAME

In this game you move the paddle by moving your mouse pointer left and right.
Your job is to hit all the bricks before you lose 3 of your lives.
You can even increase the speed of your ball using the sliding bar given on the top right.
If you hit all the bricks you win.
